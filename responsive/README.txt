Dice: Any device with less than a 650px width will display "DICE".
	These include the iPhone 4, iPhone 5, and other smaller
	devices prior to 2011.

No Dice: Any device with a screen width of more than 650px will
	display "NODICE". These devices include the larger iPads,
	Nexus 7, Samsung Galaxy S 3 and a standard desktop computer
	or laptop.
